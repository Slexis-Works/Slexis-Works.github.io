Tap the colors game

Rules: the goal of this game is to hit properly as many notes as possible. They'll appear from the left and slip to the right. Try to press the proper key when the black line is over the colored circle. The closer to the center of the circle you are, the more points you'll get. If you don't press the key in time or press the wrong key, you'll lose some life. Everytime a new color is appended, the key to press will be displayed on the circle and on the bottom right box.
